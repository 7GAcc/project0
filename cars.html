Good Cars and Bad Cars

There are lots of things to look at when you decide what car you want to buy. In most instances, you will have some sort of bias towards a specfic brand, country, feature, or even color. Keeping this in mind, here are my opinions on a few cars.

Good Cars

When I look at a car, in most cases, I treat reliability as the most important factor. After all, what is the point of owning a car if it spends most of its time in a shop? The most reliable cars, by far, are made by Toyota. The most obvious example of this is the Toyota Corolla.
